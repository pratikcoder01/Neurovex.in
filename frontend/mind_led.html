How it Works

This interface connects to a Python backend via WebSockets. The backend processes live EEG signals. When your focus score reaches or exceeds 90%, it triggers a toggle command. A debounce of 2-3 seconds prevents rapid switching. The command is also sent to the ESP32 to turn a physical LED ON or OFF.
